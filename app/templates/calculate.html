Personal Carbon Footprint Assessment

Monthly Electricity Usage (kWh): This is the amount of electricity consumed in your home or workplace during a month, measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh). It includes usage from daily appliances such as lighting, refrigerators, air conditioners, televisions, and charging devices.

Monthly Fuel Consumption (liters): This refers to the total fuel you use in a month, primarily for transportation (cars, motorcycles, etc.) and home heating systems. Examples include gasoline or diesel used for vehicles and heating oil for boilers.

Waste Generated (kg per month): This is the total weight of waste you produce in a month, typically measured in kilograms. It includes food scraps, packaging materials, paper, plastics, and other discarded items from daily life.

Monthly Water Usage (liters): This measures the water you use in a month, in liters. It includes activities such as showering, washing dishes, and doing laundry.
